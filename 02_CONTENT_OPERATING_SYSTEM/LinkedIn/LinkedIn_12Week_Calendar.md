## Strategy Notes

**Key Principles:**
1. **Sequenced, not random** — Each week builds authority
2. **Funnel-aware** — Every post moves someone down the funnel
3. **Authentic** — No fake hype, no exclamation marks
4. **Measurable** — Track every post's performance
5. **Adjustable** — If week X flops, change week X+1

**Posting Pattern:**
- **Tue or Wed morning (9 AM CET)** — Best engagement
- **Post naked text first** (2h) → Then reply with link (algorithm boost)
- **Pin top performer** to profile (1 week rotation)

**Content Mix:**
- 25% Rants (Authentic frustration + vision)
- 25% Theory (Technical authority)
- 25% Tutorials (Actionable help — inkl. Prompt Engineering)
- 15% Proof (Results + social proof)
- 10% Framing (Green IT / Gatekeeper — eingewoben, kein eigenes Format)
